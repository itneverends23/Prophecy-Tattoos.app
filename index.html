Tattoo Aftercare


  • DO NOT scratch or pick at the tattoo.
  • DO NOT go swimming or immerse your body in water (showers are fine)
  1. Take a sheet of paper towel and damp it lightly with warm water.
  2. Apply a small amount of antibacterial unscented soap on the sheet of paper towel.
  3. Gently exfoliate the skin to remove dry blood, bacterial, dead cells preventing infections
  4. Take a new paper towel and dap the tattoo dry, gently drying the tattoo
  5. Take 1cm worth of aquaphor and apply it gently to the skin
  6. Take a sheet of paper towel and wipe all the excess aquaphor (the skin absorbs all that it can in 20 min)

  • Repeat this process when the tattoo feels dry and there is a need to clean
  • AD Ointment helps cure the bottom layer of the skin but it can also take the ink out of the skin
